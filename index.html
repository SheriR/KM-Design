Design Services

Beginning a renovation project can be daunting. With more than 25 years of residential and commercial design experience, I can provide you with guidance and direction from start to finish on your project.

I have extensive professional training in design and am a member of the National Kitchen & Bath Association. In a previous life, I taught cooking classes and did small venue catering. I started designing kitchens after working in so many badly designed ones! Over the years, kitchen design has evolved into any project needing cabinetry – bathrooms, family rooms, offices.

Creating functional as well as beautiful spaces is my passion. Using standard-of-the-industry design software, I will create color renderings of your new space so you can see your project come to life before you start tearing anything down.

Need a contractor? No problem, I work with several highly qualified contractors in the area. Already have a contractor? I am happy to work with them; I work with contractors from California to New York. My goal is to make the process easy for everyone and, create something you’ll love.

Call me today and we will start moving your project from ideas to reality!
