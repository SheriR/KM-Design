Design Services

With more than 25 years of residential and commercial design experience, I am certified in kitchen design and am a member of the National Kitchen & Bath Association. In a previous life, I taught cooking classes and did small venue catering. I started designing kitchens because I got tired of working in bad ones!

Over the years, kitchen design has evolved into any project needing cabinetry – bathrooms, family rooms, offices. Space planning and creating functional as well as beautiful spaces is my passion.

Beginning a renovation project can be daunting. I can provide you with direction and guidance on a wide range of topics – from “does this color work” to “where do I start!?” Additionally, using standard-of-the-industry design software, I will create color renderings of your new space so you can see your project come to life before you start tearing anything down.
